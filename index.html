About Me

I’m Sai Srivathsav Andem, a UX Designer with a rich, interdisciplinary background rooted in architecture, design, and technology. My journey began at School of Planning and Architecture, Delhi , and continued with a Master’s in Human-Computer Interaction at DePaul University .

I began my career in 2015 as a Junior Architect, working on 2D and 3D CAD projects. After working with renowned architects in India such as Anagram, Spacematters, and QX Design, I went on to found Elsewhere Lab , a collaborative exploring design, materials, and technology.

I launched Studio Antematter in Hyderabad, where we completed architectural projects worth more than $1Million and expanded into digital design for clients like Pratyu , Krest , and the Government of Telangana .

I've earned my Master’s in HCI in 2024. I’ve since worked with RideCar and Eva Ecosystems on app and web-based dashboard design.

I’m currently seeking full-time opportunities and open to meaningful collaborations.

Outside of work, I enjoy dirt biking, painting, gym sessions, and gardening. I also love creative writing and Urdu shayari. Lately, I've found joy in reading regional language books , a surprisingly fulfilling habit.
