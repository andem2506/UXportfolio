About Me

I’m Sai Srivathsav Andem, a UX Designer with a rich, interdisciplinary background rooted in architecture, design, and technology. My journey began at School of Planning and Architecture, Delhi , and continued with a Master’s in Human-Computer Interaction at DePaul University .

I began my career in 2015 as a Junior Architect and I launched Studio Antematter in Hyderabad in 2019, where we completed design projects worth more than $1Million in 3 years and expanded into digital design for clients like Pratyu , Krest , and the Government of Telangana .

I've earned my Master’s in HCI in 2024. I’ve since worked with RideCar and Eva Ecosystems on web-design, mobile app and web-based dashboard design.

I’m currently seeking full-time opportunities and open to meaningful collaborations.
