About Me

I’m a UX Designer with a rich, interdisciplinary background rooted in architecture, design, and technology. My journey began at School of Planning and Architecture, Delhi , and continued with a Master’s in Human-Computer Interaction at DePaul University .

I started my career in 2015 with 2D and 3D CAD work. Later, I co-founded Elsewhere Lab , a collaborative exploring design, materials, and technology.

I launched Studio Antematter in Hyderabad, where we completed $1M+ in architectural projects and expanded into digital design for clients like Pratyu , Krest , and the Government of Telangana .

I've earned my Master’s in HCI in 2024. I’ve since worked with RideCar and Eva Ecosystems on app and website design.

I’m currently seeking full-time opportunities and open to meaningful collaborations.

Outside of work, I enjoy dirt biking, painting, gym sessions, and gardening. I also love creative writing and Urdu shayari. Lately, I've found joy in reading regional language books —a surprisingly fulfilling habit.
